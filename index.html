My work

eZdue

Screenshots

Description

An easy to use, card-based due-dates manager, focused on productivity and finances. Use natural language to create tasks, see them in cards, and know that is next on your to-do list. In addition, see your financial balance, where your money went in payments or purchases, and credit card info and expiration dates, in the same, easy to use, card format.

Technologies Used

Firebase for hosting, Angular 8 and Ionic 4 for front-end.

How to install

eZdue is currently a Progressive Web App, which means it runs on any modern browser and platform. Follow the instructions that show by clicking on the "How to install" label on the Settings page of the app.
